Releases jQuery UI 1.11.4 Posted on March 11, 2015 by Scott González The fourth maintenance release for jQuery UI 1.11 is out. This update brings bug fixes for Draggable, Resizable, Sortable, Accordion, Dialog, Slider, and Tooltip. For the full list of changes, see the changelog.
